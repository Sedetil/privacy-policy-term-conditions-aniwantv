Account & Data Deletion

AniWanTV allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request account deletion:

  1. Send an email to: support@aniwantv.app
  2. Email subject: Account Deletion Request - AniWanTV
  3. Include your registered email or user ID

Data that will be deleted:

  • User account information
  • Email address / username
  • Profile data
  • Favorites and watch history
  • Comments and interactions

Data that may be retained:

  • Server logs for security and fraud prevention (retained up to 30 days)

Account deletion requests will be processed within 30 days.
